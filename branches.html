USACO Training


USACO Training (Fridays After school in E203):
We train members to to compete in the algorithmic programming competition USA Computing Olympiad (USACO). This is intended primarily towards novices and USACO Bronze/Silver members. Meetings generally last from 3 - 4pm after school.
