USACO Training

Meetings


MVCS meets three times a week. Attendance to all three is NOT mandatory. You can choose to come to whichever meeting(s) interest you!

  • Creative Day (Tuesday Lunch in Back Computer Lab in MV Library) - We coordinate the collaboration between members to work together on a software application to be implemented in the real world.
  • CS Lecture Day (Wednesday Lunch in E203) - We educate members in a variety of Computer Science topics (chosen according to member interest) ranging from cryptography and networking to image processing and artificial intelligence.
  • USA Computing Olympiad (USACO) Training (Fridays After school in E203) - We train members to prepare them to compete in the algorithmic programming competition USACO. This is intended primarily toward novice and USACO Bronze/Silver members. Our meetings last roughly an hour, up to 4pm.
