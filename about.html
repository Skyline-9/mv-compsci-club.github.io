Who are we?

The Biggest And Best Tech Club of MV

MV Computer Science is, to put it bluntly, the above. Founded in 2007 as MV Java Club, MVCS (rebranded in 2013) has expanded from teaching the Java programming language to embracing the arts that are computer science, coding, and the integration of both into technologies that make our life tick.


Today, we cover topics as varied as algorithmic programming (to prep for competitions like USACO) to teaching computer science from a top-down perspective, and even web programming for educational nonprofits like Learn To Be.


We hope to not only promote tech-literacy, but also empower teens, young adults, and students to take up pursuits (and maybe even careers!) in technology. We have a lot of exciting stuff going on, and it's only going to get better from here


And we want you to be part of the journey. Sign up here.
