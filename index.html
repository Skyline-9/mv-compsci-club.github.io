About Us

MV Computer Science is, to put it bluntly, the biggest and best tech club of MV. Founded in 2007 as MV Java Club, MVCS (rebranded in 2013) has expanded from teaching the Java programming language to embracing the arts that are computer science, coding, and the integration of both into technologies that make our life tick.
